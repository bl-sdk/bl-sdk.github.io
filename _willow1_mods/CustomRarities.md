Introduces an option to modify rarities (add new ones, change their colors, time to live and etc.) in-game.


The Rarity Editor is located in mod options menu.

To have a better look at the color preview,
You can hide/show the pause menu by pressing F1.


Also features an example of a modified preset (It's not used by default) from RedxYeti’s [Character Overhaul Mod].
