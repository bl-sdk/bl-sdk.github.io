Item, kill and interactive object use tracker for use in mainly OBS. Nothing is shown on screen in game.


Keybinds for picking the enemy & interactive object you want to track


Commands:

farmhelp

newfarm [name]

listfarms

savefarm

loadfarm [name from listfarms]

trackitem [item name]

setenemy [enemy name]

setkills [kills of tracked enemy]

setitemcount [drops of tracked item]

setlegendaries [legendary drops]

setpearls [pearl drops]

wherefarm
