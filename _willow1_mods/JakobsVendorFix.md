Fixes the Jakobs vendor in the Zombie Island of Dr. Ned DLC!

![Jakobs Vendor with a better contrast UI, showing only Jakobs weapons, a grenade launcher and class mods]({{ "/assets/mods/willow1/jakobs_vendor_fix/newui.jpg" | relative_url }})
{:style="display: block; margin: 0 auto; width: 80%"}

## Features:
* No longer breaks after completing the mission
* ONLY sells Jakobs guns and class mods
* Added Jakobs grenade launchers
* More items for sale (15 + Item of the Day!)
* Higher quality items (AwesomeLevel)
* Scales to your level (mostly)
* Jakobs ClapTrap talks more often (oops)
